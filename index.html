ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TopRacer Range

Experience the future of golf technology with TopRacer Range, where precision meets excitement. Whether you're honing your skills or enjoying a friendly competition, elevate your game with cutting-edge tracking and analysis capabilities. It's golfing innovation at its finest.

Adventure Golf

Embark on a thrilling journey through our Adventure Golf course, where excitement and challenge await at every hole. Immerse yourself in captivating landscapes and navigate through fun obstacles as you putt your way to victory. It's an unforgettable golfing adventure for players of all ages and skill levels.

Golf Lessons

Unlock your full potential with our expert-led golf lessons, tailored to players of all abilities. Whether you're a beginner looking to master the basics or a seasoned pro aiming to refine your technique, our experienced instructors are here to guide you every step of the way. Discover the joy of improvement and elevate your game with personalized instruction in a supportive and encouraging environment.
